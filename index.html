invisalign Open Day

If you would like to see first hand the transformational results that Invisalign can deliver, why not reserve your spot at our Invisalign event here at Hitchin Dental on 18th June 2022? Simply fill out the form below and someone will be in touch within 48 hours to arrange the best time for your free consultation.

When you attend our exclusive Invisalign Open Day you will receive a full Invisalign consultation free of charge and also get a fascinating 3-D intra-oral scan done which will reveal your potential future smile thanks to Invisalign – what a game changer! All attendees will qualify for some amazing benefits and discounts too which will help make your Invisalign journey more affordable. If you sign up for your Invisalign treatment during the event you will have the following add-ons included:

  • FREE teeth whitening worth £350
  • SAVE up to £550 on retainers
  • £150 BONUS discount on treatment cost

If you would like to spread the cost of your Invisalign treatment, please ask reception about our special Invisalign 0% finance plan. We’d be happy to answer any questions you may have about the Invisalign event, so please go ahead and fill in your details below and one of our great team will be in touch really soon.
